PBOC injects net 2170 billion yuan via 7-day reverse repo at 1.40%

On 13 July 2026, the People's Bank of China conducted a 2240 billion yuan 7-day reverse repo operation at a fixed rate of 1.40%, with 70 billion yuan maturing, resulting in a net injection of 2170 billion yuan.
